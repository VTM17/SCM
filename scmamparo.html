Who We Are?

S. C. Mamparo Enterprise was established in 1987 as Rabbi Electrical. Its main thrust 33 years ago was to pursue excellence in the field of electrical works and installation, and electro-mechanical systems. Nevertheless, the company has revolutionized to undergo paradigm shift in the last couple of decades and has drastically responded to the demands of the industrializing metropolis.

We strive to maintain the highest standards while exceeding client’s expectations at all levels.
